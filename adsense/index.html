About Google AdSense

Google AdSense is a program run by Google that allows website publishers to serve advertisements. These ads are generally served based on content, user behavior, and other factors that Google deems relevant.

Privacy & Cookie Information

When you visit HJTrending or other sites that use Google AdSense:

  • Google may set cookies on your browser to collect information about your browsing behavior
  • This information helps Google serve you more relevant ads
  • You can control these settings through Google Ad Settings
  • You can also opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative
